Project Spotlight: FitFleet - An app enabling users to deliver items on foot while staying fit through gamification

Fitfleet is focussed to promote fitness, reduce carbon emissions, and alleviate traffic congestion in urban areas, by incentivizing walking deliveries through gamification

Project Information

  • Category: New product ideas
  • Company: Personal
  • Date: May 2025
  • Project URL: Coming soon

Impact through incentivizing walking deliveries

To add fuel to my curious product mind, I am always on the lookout for problems requiring simple solutions. The concept of an app for users to deliver items on foot, motivated by fitness gamification and environmental benefits, appears to occupy a relatively unexplored niche in the delivery market. The current technological solutions don't address the fitness motivation aspect that could make an on-foot delivery app attractive to health-conscious individuals looking to earn money while exercising. While elements of the proposed concept exist in various forms across the market, the specific combination of on-foot delivery with fitness gamification appears to be a novel approach that could potentially find a niche in the competitive delivery service landscape.
